Our Logo

"The Red Rooster has been a beloved symbol of the franchise since 1973. The original logo featured a cartoon rooster with a can of Red Rooster chips. The logo has been updated several times over the years, but the rooster has always remained the central figure. The Red Rooster logo is a reminder of the chain's humble beginnings and its commitment to quality products."

Tagline

The tagline "Harvested Fresh, Delivered Best" was created through a process focused on the franchise's core values of freshness, quality, and reliable delivery. Considering a diverse product range, the tagline encapsulates the high-quality nature of vegetables, fruits, milk, bread, eggs, and meat. It emphasizes both freshness and delivery excellence, aiming for memorability through simplicity. The tagline aligns with the customer experience, assuring peak freshness and careful delivery. Crafted with active verbs, it serves as a call to action, inviting customers to envision the journey from farm to doorstep. Likely subjected to market testing, the tagline's finalization represents a powerful commitment to quality and reliability in product delivery.

History

In 1985, the Johnson family transformed their picturesque farm into the Red Rooster Farm Shop, a haven for fresh produce and meats. Fueled by a passion for sustainable farming, they aimed to provide their community with top-quality, locally sourced goods.

The iconic red rooster logo quickly became a symbol of their commitment to freshness. As demand soared, the first franchise opened in the early 1990s, spreading the Red Rooster ethos. Each franchise maintained the family's dedication to ethical farming.

Over the years, the Red Rooster Farm Shop evolved to offer a diverse range of products, including dairy, cheeses, and fish. It became a community hub, connecting locals with farmers and artisans. The franchise, with its blend of tradition and modern convenience, continues to thrive, embodying a legacy built on quality and community values.
